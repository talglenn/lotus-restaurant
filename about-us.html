About Us

Lotus Chinese Restaurant offers delicious dining, takeout and delivery to Atlanta, GA.

Lotus Chinese Restaurant is a cornerstone in the Atlanta community and has been recognized for its outstanding Chinese cuisine, excellent service and friendly staff.

Our Chinese restaurant is known for its modern interpretation of classic dishes and its insistence on only using high quality fresh ingredients.
